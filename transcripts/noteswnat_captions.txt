Memory hobbies that activate your hippocampus (reading, crosswords, journaling). And creative hobbies for fine motor skills (knitting, painting, embroidery). When you’re doing hobbies from each category you’re not just keeping busy, you’re protecting your brain from aging. The key is to have variety and do something challenging.
