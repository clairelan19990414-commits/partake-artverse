@vinny_creative notes

    Thesis:
    @vinny_creative turns design education into a ladder from taste-gathering to strategic brand work. The account contrasts ordinary graphic design with higher-value brand design, arguing that AI and templates make technical execution cheap while judgment, taste, systems thinking, and cultural research remain defensible.

    Key concepts:
    - Brand Designer vs Graphic Designer: A distinction between making individual visuals and building strategic systems, workflows, research, and client-ready brand logic. (via DXbmM2BDLBm, DXZPK4bDIUR, DXRBvNEDnoZ)
- AI-Proof Design Judgment: The claim that designers survive automation by developing taste, strategy, and brand thinking rather than relying on execution alone. (via DXenjphjDV9, DXeP9HmDNxI)
- Reference Infrastructure: Pinterest, Cosmos, ShotDeck, Magnum, The Met, Brand New, Are.na, and Cannes Lions are framed as systems for building visual intelligence. (via DXPAi2lDI_8, DXRgB5cDLvy, DXWYgFZDHTS)
- Tool Stack Stratification: Paid/free resource lists and graphic/brand designer comparisons show tools as markers of maturity, workflow, and professional ambition. (via DXW82GLjEDV, DXRBvNEDnoZ, DXeO8kdDCiB)
- Visual Style Taxonomies: Repeated style-list posts create a shared vocabulary for recognizing and collecting visual languages. (via DXIP-XSgXX7, DXG6XOnDDw0, DXb9HeBjMF9)
