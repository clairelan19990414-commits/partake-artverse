    @stylebykvn frames fashion as a field guide to place: neighborhoods, museums, flea markets, Olympic uniforms, jewelry houses, and local brands become ways to read culture geographically. The account treats style as travel research, linking garments and accessories to cities, subcultures, national identity, and the infrastructures of shopping.
