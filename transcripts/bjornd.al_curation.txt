@bjornd.al curation

    Core thesis:
    @bjornd.al is a Minneapolis-based multi-hyphenate (musician, graphic designer, writer, video essayist) who uses absurdist humor as the connective tissue across wildly disparate creative output. His primary vehicle is "Esoteric Meme Breakdown," a video series that uses internet memes as entry points into obscure history, conspiracy lore, and art criticism. Simultaneously, he documents a dense local DIY scene through band promotion, self-published chapbooks, invented holidays, and hand-made merchandise — all delivered with a deadpan, self-aware voice.
