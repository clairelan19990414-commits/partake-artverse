DXOqtDlDMYQ | 5 STEP BLUEPRINT 👇 | 5 STEP BLUEPRINT 👇

Step 1 — MASTER ONE TOOL

I would pick one design tool and learn it deeply. Start with Figma or Illustrator.

Step 2 — REVERSE ENGINEER GREAT DESIGN

Instead of just looking at inspiration, break designs down.

Why this typography?
Why this layout?
Why this color?

Step 3 — BUILD A DAILY DESIGN HABIT

When I started design, I spent a whole year making posters and graphics every single day just to build the skill. Set yourself up for a challenge, because consistency beats talent.

Step 4 — LEARN HOW DESIGN WORKS FOR BUSINESS

Study branding, positioning and communication. True design solves problems, not just being pretty.

Step 5 — PUBLISH EVERYTHING

Post every project online. This way you would see your progress over time and other people could hire you.
