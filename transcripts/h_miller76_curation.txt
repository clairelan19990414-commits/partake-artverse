    Core thesis:
    Harry Miller is a New York-based poet and personal trainer who fuses physical discipline, spoken word, and spiritual philosophy into a unified practice of self-overcoming. His central argument is that wisdom, poetry, and bodily strength are all forms of warfare — tools for confronting inner demons, societal indifference, and existential despair. He channels personal experience with depression and suicide into a public advocacy posture, positioning resilience as prophetic and the struggle itself as the path to victory.
